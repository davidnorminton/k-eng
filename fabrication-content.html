Fabrication

The team of highly skilled engineers are devoted to ensuring the very best quality in product manufacture and assembly.

We use a wide range of techniques including Mig welding, Tig welding, Pulse Tig Welding, Stud Welding amongst many others. Each of these techniques can be applied to various materials.

Our 15-tonne crane, which runs the full length of the building, allows a smooth flow of work around the workshop. We can undertake repair work, prototypes, bespoke developments and production runs.

One of the other aspects of our business is site works.We have mobile equipment for undertaking off-site workings. This enables us to install parts which have been pre-fabricated in our workshop and brought to site for final fitting.

We have the ability to design and manufacture various components which can be applied to sheet metal parts such as bosses, pipe fittings etc.
