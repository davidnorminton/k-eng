Folding Press

Our Bystronic and Edwards Pearson presses ensure the highest standard of precision folding.

The folding operation of sheet metal product is carried out to a very high level of quality engineering.

The two hydraulic press brake machines each have six axes allowing for constant repeatable bending to create a precise and professional result. With a wide range of tooling, each is capable of folding between 0.5mm and 6mm and handling up to 3 metres in length.

The technology of the press machines includes a CNC graphic interface and a computerised library. The library gives easy access to our customer database so that when repeat orders are received we can quickly and efficiently retrieve past details. This helps us re-create previous work to the same high standard whilst at the same time being able to pass on savings to our customers.
